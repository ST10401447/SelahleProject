Shonisani Selahle

As a Chartered Management Accountant (CIMA), Shonisani brings over 15 years of experience in Environmental, Occupational Health and Safety Consulting. She holds the LLB, CMA (CIMA), and MBA qualifications. Her expertise spans risk assessment, compliance auditing, and strategic environmental planning. She is currently pursuing an Honors in Environmental Management. Shonisani is registered with South African Council for Project and Construction Management Professions.

Fortune Selahle

Fortune holds certificates in Accounting Skills, Crime Risk Management, OHS P1, with over 8 years of experience in Environmental, Health and Safety Consulting. His background includes extensive work in mining operations, industrial safety protocols, and health system environments. His attention to detail and systematic approach to compliance makes him invaluable to our clients. Fortune is currently pursuing his BSc degree in Environmental Management and is focusing on service quality and efficiency.

Zwivhuya Ramavu

Zwivhuya holds a BSc Degree in Geology and Microbiology with over 5 years of experience in Environmental Management and Health & Safety consulting. She specializes in project management, stakeholder engagement, and regulatory compliance. Her technical expertise combined with strong communication skills makes her an effective project coordinator and client liaison. She has worked extensively with mining companies, consulting firms, SOEs, NGO's, and environmental regulatory bodies.

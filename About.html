About Selahle Consultancy and Projects

Background

Selahle Consultancy and Projects (PTY) Ltd was established in 2014 and is 100% Black-Owned. Member and/or directors are from a previously disadvantaged background in Limpopo Province. With direct access to a network of technically qualified and experienced Environmental Consultants (as well as OHS consultants) and IT Analysts with the required equipment resources and systems. SC Projects has the edge to provide clients with an appropriate solution for their needs.

Approach

To bring fresh meaning into the consulting field by achieving the unachievable. To meet the client's expertise and delivering the expected on time with no further budget implications and adding no harm to human lives and the environment.
